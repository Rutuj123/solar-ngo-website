ABOUT US

Shivam Jaljivan Foundation

Shivam Jaljivan Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to improving rural lives through sustainable solutions in clean water and solar energy. We aim to provide affordable and innovative systems that ensure access to safe drinking water and reliable energy for every village.

Through technology, awareness and community partnerships, we work toward building healthier and self-sustaining rural communities.

🌍

Our Vision

To create a future where every village has access to clean drinking water and renewable energy.

🎯

Our Mission

  • Install solar-powered water purification systems
  • Promote renewable energy in rural areas
  • Spread awareness about water conservation
  • Provide affordable community solutions
WHAT WE DO

Sustainable Rural Water Solutions

We work on installing solar-based water filtration machines and supporting rural communities with sustainable, low-cost and long-term solutions.

WHY IT MATTERS

Creating Better Health & Better Lives

Many rural communities still lack access to safe drinking water and electricity. Our work helps bridge this gap by improving public health, reducing hardship, and creating a better quality of life for families and future generations.
