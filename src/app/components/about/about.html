About Us

Shivam Jaljivan Foundation

Shivam Jaljivan Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to improving rural lives through sustainable solutions in clean water and solar energy. We aim to provide affordable and innovative systems that ensure access to safe drinking water and reliable energy for every village.

Our Vision

To create a future where every village has access to clean drinking water and renewable energy.

Our Mission

  • To install solar-powered water purification systems
  • To promote renewable energy in rural areas
  • To spread awareness about water conservation
  • To provide affordable solutions for communities

What We Do

We work on installing solar-based water filtration machines and supporting rural communities with sustainable and low-cost solutions.

Why It Matters

Many rural communities still lack access to safe drinking water and electricity. Our work helps bridge this gap and improves overall health and quality of life.
